# Research brief — SaaS pricing models for an early-stage launch

_2026-06-18 · for the fintech client_

**Bottom line:** For this launch, usage-based pricing will stall early adoption — buyers can't predict their bill before they trust the product. A simple 3-tier seat model with a generous free tier converts faster pre-PMF. Revisit usage-based once expansion revenue matters.

## Key findings

- **Seat-based tiers reduce buyer math at evaluation** — a fixed price per seat is easy to approve; usage-based forces a forecast the buyer can't make yet. [source: illustrative]
- **Usage-based wins on expansion** but raises perceived risk before product-market fit, lengthening the first sale. [source: illustrative]
- **A free tier** lowers the activation barrier for self-serve SaaS and feeds the top of the funnel; pair it with a clear upgrade trigger. [source: illustrative]
